# Astrology Glossary - Western, Vedic & Numerology Terms

## A

**Ascendant (Rising Sign)** - The zodiac sign rising on the eastern horizon at your birth moment; represents how you present yourself to the world and your physical appearance.

**Aspect** - Angular relationship between planets in a birth chart (conjunction, square, trine, opposition, sextile); indicates how planetary energies interact.

**Atmakaraka** - (Vedic) The planet at the highest degree in your chart; represents your soul's desire and primary karmic lessons.

**Ayanamsa** - (Vedic) The difference between tropical and sidereal zodiacs; currently about 24 degrees, used to calculate Vedic charts from Western positions.

## B

**Birth Chart (Natal Chart)** - Map of planetary positions at the exact moment and location of your birth; foundation of personalized astrology readings.

**Bhava** - (Vedic) House in Vedic astrology; 12 divisions representing life areas like career, relationships, health.

**Bhukti** - (Vedic) Sub-period within a larger dasha period; refines timing predictions.

## C

**Cardinal Signs** - Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn; initiators who start new cycles and lead change.

**Conjunction** - When two or more planets occupy the same degree in a chart; blends their energies intensely.

**Cusp** - The dividing line between two zodiac signs or houses; sensitive points in a chart.

## D

**Dasha** - (Vedic) Planetary period system unique to Vedic astrology; predicts timing of major life events based on planetary rulership periods.

**Descendant** - Point opposite your Ascendant; represents partnership, marriage, and how you relate to others.

**Decan** - 10-degree subdivision of each zodiac sign; adds flavor to sun sign interpretations.

**Debilitation** - When a planet occupies its weakest zodiac sign; challenges that planet's expression (e.g., Moon in Scorpio).

## E

**Eclipse** - When Sun and Moon align with lunar nodes; accelerates fate and brings sudden changes.

**Elements** - Fire, Earth, Air, Water; fundamental energies that group zodiac signs by temperament.

**Exaltation** - When a planet occupies its strongest zodiac sign; enhances that planet's expression (e.g., Sun in Aries).

**Expression Number** - (Numerology) Calculated from your full birth name; reveals natural talents and how you express yourself.

## F

**Fixed Signs** - Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius; stabilizers who sustain what cardinals start.

## G

**Gochara** - (Vedic) Planetary transits; current sky positions affecting your natal chart.

**Graha** - (Vedic) Planet; literally "that which grasps" in Sanskrit, referring to celestial bodies.

## H

**Houses** - 12 divisions of the birth chart representing life areas: self, money, communication, home, creativity, health, partnership, transformation, philosophy, career, community, spirituality.

## I

**IC (Imum Coeli)** - Bottom of the chart; represents home, family roots, and emotional foundation.

## J

**Jyotish** - Vedic astrology; Sanskrit term meaning "science of light."

**Jupiter Return** - When transiting Jupiter returns to its natal position (every ~12 years); brings expansion and new opportunities.

## K

**Kala Sarpa Yoga** - (Vedic) When all planets fall between Rahu and Ketu; creates intense karmic experiences and challenges.

**Karaka** - (Vedic) Significator planet; shows what each planet represents (e.g., Venus = relationships, Jupiter = wisdom).

**Ketu** - (Vedic) South Node of the Moon; represents past life karma, spiritual detachment, and what you're releasing.

**Kundali** - (Vedic) Birth chart; also called horoscope or Janma Kundali.

## L

**Lagnam** - (Vedic) Ascendant; rising sign at birth.

**Life Path Number** - (Numerology) Calculated from birth date; reveals your life's purpose and major lessons.

**Lunar Nodes** - Rahu (North Node) and Ketu (South Node); karmic points where eclipses occur.

## M

**Mahadasha** - (Vedic) Major planetary period in dasha system; lasts from 6 to 20 years depending on the planet.

**Malefic** - Traditionally challenging planets (Mars, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu); bring difficulties that catalyze growth.

**Master Numbers** - (Numerology) 11, 22, 33; carry heightened spiritual significance and power.

**MC (Midheaven)** - Top of the chart; represents career, public image, and life direction.

**Modalities** - Cardinal, Fixed, Mutable; show how zodiac signs express energy and handle change.

**Moon Sign** - Zodiac sign where the Moon was located at your birth; represents emotions, instincts, and inner self.

**Mutable Signs** - Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces; adapters who facilitate transitions and bring flexibility.

## N

**Nakshatra** - (Vedic) Lunar mansion; 27 divisions of the zodiac based on Moon's position, each with unique qualities and ruling deities.

**Natal Chart** - See Birth Chart.

**Nodes** - See Lunar Nodes.

## O

**Opposition** - 180-degree aspect between planets; creates tension requiring balance between opposite energies.

**Orb** - Allowable degree range for an aspect to be considered active (typically 6-10 degrees).

## P

**Panchanga** - (Vedic) Five-limb almanac showing daily tithi, nakshatra, yoga, karana, and vara for timing activities.

**Planetary Dignity** - Strength of a planet based on zodiac sign placement (exaltation, domicile, detriment, fall).

**Pluto Return** - When transiting Pluto returns to natal position (~248 years); transformation for nations and collective.

**Progressed Chart** - Advanced technique showing your evolving chart as you age; one day of life equals one year.

## Q

**Quadrants** - Four main angles of the chart (Ascendant, IC, Descendant, MC); foundational points.

## R

**Rahu** - (Vedic) North Node of the Moon; represents worldly desires, future karma, and what you're moving toward.

**Rashi** - (Vedic) Zodiac sign; 12 divisions of the zodiac in Vedic astrology.

**Retrograde** - When a planet appears to move backward in the sky; internal processing of that planet's themes.

**Rising Sign** - See Ascendant.

## S

**Sade Sati** - (Vedic) Seven-and-a-half year period when Saturn transits your Moon sign and adjacent signs; challenging but transformative.

**Saturn Return** - When transiting Saturn returns to natal position (~29.5 years); major life restructuring and maturation.

**Sextile** - 60-degree aspect between planets; creates opportunities and ease.

**Sidereal Zodiac** - (Vedic) Star-based zodiac aligned with actual constellations; used in Vedic astrology.

**Square** - 90-degree aspect between planets; creates tension and challenges that drive growth.

**Sun Sign** - Zodiac sign where the Sun was located at your birth; represents core identity and life purpose.

## T

**Tithi** - (Vedic) Lunar day in Hindu calendar; important for timing rituals and events.

**Transit** - Current position of planets in the sky affecting your natal chart; triggers events and experiences.

**Trine** - 120-degree aspect between planets; creates harmony, talent, and natural flow.

**Tropical Zodiac** - (Western) Season-based zodiac starting at Spring Equinox (0° Aries); used in Western astrology.

## U

**Upachaya Houses** - (Vedic) Houses 3, 6, 10, 11; improve over time and benefit from malefic planets.

## V

**Varga Charts** - (Vedic) Divisional charts (D-9, D-10, etc.); examine specific life areas in detail.

**Vimshottari Dasha** - (Vedic) 120-year planetary period system; most common dasha system used for predictions.

## Y

**Yoga** - (Vedic) Planetary combination creating specific results (e.g., Raja Yoga = success, Dhana Yoga = wealth).

## Z

**Zenith** - Highest point in the sky; related to MC (Midheaven) in chart.

**Zodiac** - Belt of sky divided into 12 signs; foundation of Western and Vedic astrology.
