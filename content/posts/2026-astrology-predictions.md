## 2026 Astrology FAQs

### When does Neptune enter Aries in 2026?

Neptune enters Aries on **January 26, 2026** at 12:39 AM EST. However, Neptune will briefly retrograde back into Pisces from October 13, 2026 to January 26, 2027, giving us one final Pisces recap before the full Aries era begins.

### How many Mercury retrogrades are there in 2026?

There are **three Mercury retrograde periods in 2026:** March 2-25 (Pisces/Aquarius), July 4-28 (Cancer), and October 29 - November 19 (Scorpio/Libra). Notably, all three retrogrades occur primarily in water signs.

### What is the most significant eclipse of 2026?

The **August 12, 2026 Annular Solar Eclipse at 19¬∞ Leo** is considered the most impactful. This eclipse begins the new Leo-Aquarius eclipse series and activates themes of heart-centered leadership, creative self-expression, and individual destiny.

### Which zodiac signs will be most affected by 2026 astrology?

**Aries, Leo, Aquarius, and Pisces** will feel 2026's shifts most intensely. Aries due to Neptune's ingress, Leo and Aquarius due to the eclipse axis shift, and Pisces due to Neptune's final passage through their sign.

### Is 2026 a good year for starting new relationships?

The first half of 2026 is generally favorable for new relationships, especially May-September before Venus retrograde begins. Avoid starting significant new relationships during Venus retrograde (October 3 - November 15), but use this time to heal past relationship patterns.

### What should I do during Neptune in Aries?

Embrace spiritual courage, take action on your dreams, pioneer new practices, and be willing to reinvent yourself. Neptune in Aries rewards those who combine vision with initiative. However, watch for spiritual bypassing, ego-driven "enlightenment," or reckless idealism.

### How do I find my 2026 horoscope for my specific sign?

Your sun sign forecast provides a general overview, but for personalized insights, check your **Rising Sign** horoscope as well. For the most accurate predictions, consult your complete birth chart and note where the key 2026 transits fall in your personal houses.
