Every Aries has a moment (or a decade) where they're basically a human wrecking ball. The shadow side of all that Mars energy is **aggression without wisdom**â€”acting first, thinking never. Here's the uncomfortable truth: Aries can be selfish, not because they don't care about others, but because they genuinely forget other people have different speeds, needs, and internal lives.
