## Capricorn Compatibility: Who Can Climb Alongside?

**Capricorn + Taurus**: Stable, loyal, building together. Both are earth signs who value security, quality, and tangible results. Taurus softens Capricorn's edges; Capricorn gives Taurus structure. The challenge? Both can be stubborn and resistant to necessary change.

**Capricorn + Virgo**: Practical, organized, mutually supportive. Both value competence, improvement, and service. Virgo handles details; Capricorn handles strategy. Great partnership for building businesses or stable lives. The risk? Mutual anxiety and criticism without outside emotional input.

**Capricorn + Scorpio**: Power couple potential. Both are ambitious, strategic, and value loyalty. Scorpio brings emotional depth; Capricorn brings stability. They respect each other's strength. The challenge? Both are controlling and can create power struggles instead of partnership.

**Capricorn + Pisces**: Opposite energies that can balance beautifully. Pisces brings creativity and emotional depth; Capricorn brings structure and grounding. Pisces softens Capricorn; Capricorn manifests Pisces' dreams. The risk? Capricorn finds Pisces impractical; Pisces finds Capricorn cold.

**Capricorn + Aries**: Painful mismatch. Aries' impulsivity terrifies Capricorn. Capricorn's caution frustrates Aries. Aries leaps; Capricorn plans. Can work in business if roles are clear, but in romance, the friction is constant unless both are exceptionally evolved.

**Capricorn + Libra**: Awkward energy. Libra's indecision drives Capricorn insane. Capricorn's intensity overwhelms Libra's need for lightness. Libra wants romance and aesthetics; Capricorn wants functionality. Different values, different pacing. Challenging without significant compromise.
