### Neptune Enters Aries: Your 11th House Awakens (January 26, 2026)

This is the transit that changes everything.

Neptune hasn't been in Aries since 1862. Nobody alive has experienced this energy. And for Gemini, Neptune moving from your 10th house (career, public image) into your 11th house (friends, communities, hopes, and dreams) marks a profound shift in where you seek meaning.

- Unclear professional boundaries

**Your 11th House Neptune Action Plan:**

- Vet new groups carefully before committing
- Channel dreams through collaborative projects

- Trust your intuition about people, but verify

**Key Dates:**

### Mercury Retrograde 2026: Three Cycles, Three Opportunities

Okay, Gemini—this is your moment to shine.

Everyone else panics during Mercury retrograde. Memes flood the internet. People blame missed flights and broken phones on the backward motion of your ruling planet.

But you? You're built for this.

Mercury rules Gemini. Its retrograde cycles are times when your natural ability to see multiple perspectives, revisit old ideas, and communicate with nuance becomes a superpower. While others stumble, you navigate.

**The 2026 Mercury Retrograde Calendar:**

| Retrograde Period | Signs | Your Theme |
|-------------------|-------|------------|
| **March 2-25** | Pisces → Aquarius | Career reflection, then community reassessment |
| **July 4-28** | Cancer | Money, values, and self-worth review |
| **October 29 - November 19** | Scorpio → Libra | Deep healing, then creative/romantic revision |

#### Mercury Retrograde #1: March 2-25 (Pisces/Aquarius)

This retrograde begins in your 10th house of career (Pisces) and slides back into your 9th house (Aquarius). Translation: you're revisiting professional decisions and big-picture beliefs.

**Practical suggestions:**
- Review your career path—are you where you want to be?
- Reconnect with mentors or teachers from your past
- Revisit abandoned educational or publishing projects
- Don't sign major contracts until after March 25

#### Mercury Retrograde #2: July 4-28 (Cancer)

Cancer rules your 2nd house of money, values, and self-worth. This is the retrograde where your bank account and belief in yourself come under review.

**Practical suggestions:**
- Audit your finances (you might find forgotten subscriptions or owed money)
- Reconsider your pricing if you're self-employed
- Examine what you truly value vs. what you've been told to value
- Past income sources may resurface—in good or challenging ways

#### Mercury Retrograde #3: October 29 - November 19 (Scorpio/Libra)

The final retrograde of 2026 moves through your 6th house (Scorpio) and 5th house (Libra). Health routines and creative/romantic matters are up for revision.

**Practical suggestions:**
- Revisit health protocols that stopped working
- Old lovers or creative projects may reappear
- Journal about what brings you genuine joy
- Avoid major health decisions until after November 19

**Your Mercury Retrograde Mastery Guide:**

Since Mercury is your ruler, you have an advantage others don't. Use these periods for:
- **Re-reading:** Books you missed things in the first time
- **Re-connecting:** People you've lost touch with
- **Re-working:** Projects that stalled
- **Re-flecting:** On patterns you keep repeating
- **Re-charging:** Your mental batteries

Don't fight these cycles. Flow with them. They're designed for minds like yours.
