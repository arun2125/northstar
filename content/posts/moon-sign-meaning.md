You know your Sun sign. Everyone does. It's the first thing people ask about and the first thing astrology columns address.

But here's a secret most people don't know: **your Moon sign is probably more accurate.**

If you've ever read your horoscope and thought "this doesn't sound like me at all," your Moon sign is probably why. Let's talk about it.

---

## Sun Sign vs. Moon Sign

**Your Sun sign** is who you're becoming. It's your conscious identity, your ego, the self you're growing into over your lifetime.

**Your Moon sign** is who you already are. It's your emotional core, your instincts, the you that comes out when you're not performing.

The Sun is your highlight reel. The Moon is your private journal.

(If you've never related to your Sun sign, [here's why](/blog/why-not-like-your-sun-sign).)

---

## Why Moon Signs Are So Accurate

Your Moon sign describes:
- How you process emotions
- What you need to feel safe
- Your instinctive reactions
- How you nurture and want to be nurtured
- What comforts you
- Your relationship with your mother/maternal figures
- What "home" means to you
- How you behave in private

This is the stuff that actually runs your daily experience. While your Sun sign represents your conscious goals, your Moon sign represents your unconscious needs.

Guess which one affects you more on a random Tuesday?

## How to Find Your Moon Sign

Your Moon sign depends on your birth time, not just your birth date. The Moon changes signs every 2-3 days, so being born at 2 AM versus 2 PM could mean completely different Moon signs.

You need:
- Your birth date
- Your exact birth time (or as close as possible)
- Your birth location

Plug these into any birth chart calculator, and look for the Moon symbol (‚òΩ).
