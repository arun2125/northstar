What are you here to learn?

Not what you're good at — you know that already. What feels uncomfortable, unfamiliar, but somehow right when you lean into it?

That's your North Node.

---

## What the Nodes Are

The Lunar Nodes aren't planets — they're mathematical points where the Moon's orbit crosses the Sun's path. They're always exactly opposite each other.

**North Node (☊):** What you're growing toward. Your soul's curriculum this lifetime. It feels uncomfortable but fulfilling.

**South Node (☋):** What you're coming from. Skills and patterns from past lives (or early life). It feels familiar but limiting.

The Nodes work as a pair. You can't understand one without the other.

---

## The Basic Pattern

Your South Node is your comfort zone. You're naturally good at it because you've already learned it. But staying there keeps you stuck.

Your North Node is your growth edge. It feels awkward and scary. But moving toward it is why you're here.

Think of it like this:
- South Node = Your PhD. You're already an expert.
- North Node = Your freshman class. Everything to learn.

The lesson? You don't need another PhD. You need to become a beginner again.

## Working With Your Nodes

### 1. Notice Your South Node Patterns

When you're stressed, you probably default to South Node behavior. Notice it without judgment.

"There I go, people-pleasing again" (South Node Libra).
"There I go, trying to control everything" (South Node Capricorn).

Awareness is the first step.

### 2. Lean Into Discomfort

Your North Node will feel uncomfortable. That's how you know you're growing.

The awkwardness of asserting yourself (North Node Aries), expressing creativity (North Node Leo), or surrendering control (North Node Pisces) — that discomfort is growth.

### 3. Don't Abandon Your South Node

You don't have to reject your South Node gifts. You just can't live there.

A Scorpio South Node still gets to be deep and intense — just not as a way of avoiding Taurus's simplicity. A Capricorn South Node still gets to achieve — just not as a way of avoiding Cancer's vulnerability.

### 4. Follow the Pull

Even when it's uncomfortable, your North Node usually feels *right*. There's a pull toward it, a sense of purpose when you engage it.

Pay attention to that pull. Follow it.

## Finding Your Nodes

Your Nodes depend on your birth date (not time). They move backward through the zodiac and stay in each sign for about 18 months.

Look up your birth chart and find the symbols ☊ (North Node) and ☋ (South Node).
