## Sagittarius at Work: The Visionary Wanderer

In their careers, Sagittarius needs meaning, autonomy, and room to grow. They're not built for cubicles, rigid hierarchies, or doing the same thing for forty years.

You'll find Sagittarius excelling in:
- Teaching and academia (sharing knowledge, inspiring growth)
- Travel and tourism (exploring the world)
- Philosophy and religion (seeking meaning)
- Publishing and media (spreading ideas)
- Coaching and motivational speaking (inspiring expansion)
- International work (bridging cultures)
- Entrepreneurship (freedom and vision)
- Any role that offers autonomy, learning, and purpose

Sagittarius brings vision to their work. They see the big picture, the future possibility, the larger meaning. They're also natural teachers. Sagittarius loves sharing what they've learned, making complex ideas accessible, helping others grow. They're enthusiastic educators who make learning feel like adventure rather than obligation.

But Sagittarius at work struggles with:
- Follow-through and finishing projects
- Dealing with administrative details and routine tasks
- Staying in roles once they've mastered them
- Working within systems they don't believe in
- Being managed or micromanaged

Sagittarius can be the person with a dozen half-finished projects, five business ideas, three certifications they never completed. They get the vision, launch with enthusiasm, then lose interest once it requires sustained, unglamorous effort. They're perpetual starters, terrible finishers.

They can also struggle with authority. If Sagittarius doesn't respect their manager or believe in the company's mission, they'll either push back constantly or leave. They can't fake alignment. They need to believe what they're doing matters.

Evolved Sagittarius learns to channel their vision into sustained effort. They find work that allows enough variety and autonomy to keep them engaged while building something real. They learn that mastery requires commitment, and that finishing what you start is its own kind of freedom.
