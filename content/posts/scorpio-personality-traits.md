## The Scorpio Archetype: All or Nothing

Scorpio doesn't do surface-level anything. Co-ruled by Mars (the warrior planet) and Pluto (lord of the underworld, death, and rebirth), Scorpio is **built for intensity, transformation, and psychological warfare** if you cross them.

If you want sunshine and easy breezy vibes, date a Sagittarius. Scorpio is the friend who helps you hide a body (metaphorically... mostly). They're loyal to the grave, but getting into their inner circle requires passing tests you didn't know you were taking. Every interaction is a depth check. Can you handle real conversation? Can you sit with darkness without trying to fix it? Can you keep a secret like your life depends on it? Because in Scorpio's world, trust is currency, and they're not handing it out for free.

Here's the thing about Scorpio that pop astrology gets wrong: **they're not mysterious for aesthetic reasons**. Underneath that cool exterior is someone who feels *everything* at 11/10 intensity. They just refuse to let you see them bleed. The famous "Scorpio stare"? That's them scanning for threats, calculating whether you're safe, and deciding if you're worth the risk of letting in.
