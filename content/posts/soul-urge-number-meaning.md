# Soul Urge Number: Discover What Your Heart Truly Wants

Beneath your outer personality and life path lies something deeper—your true desires, your authentic motivations, the things that make your soul sing. This is the domain of your Soul Urge Number, also known as your Heart's Desire Number.

While your Life Path Number reveals the journey you're meant to walk and your Expression Number shows the talents you'll use, your Soul Urge Number answers a more intimate question: What does your heart truly want? What fulfills you at the deepest level? What would you pursue if no one was watching and nothing was stopping you?

Your Soul Urge Number is the voice of your authentic self—the you that exists beneath all the conditioning, expectations, and roles you play. It's calculated from the vowels in your birth name, as vowels represent the inner sound, the breath, the soul's expression.

This comprehensive guide will teach you how to calculate and understand your Soul Urge Number, helping you honor your deepest desires and live from your authentic core.

## What Is the Soul Urge Number?

Your Soul Urge Number (Heart's Desire Number) represents:

**Your Deepest Desires**: What you genuinely long for, not what you think you should want or what others expect of you.

**Your Inner Motivation**: What drives you from within. Why you do what you do. Your authentic fuel.

**What Fulfills You**: Activities, experiences, and expressions that make you feel most alive and satisfied.

**Your Secret Self**: The you that exists in your private moments, when no one is watching. Your unfiltered desires and dreams.

**Your Emotional Needs**: What you need to feel emotionally satisfied and at peace with yourself.

Unlike your Life Path (your soul's journey) or Expression Number (your talents), your Soul Urge operates primarily in your inner world. Other people might not see it, but you feel it constantly. It's the whisper of your authentic self, saying "This is what I truly want."

When your outer life aligns with your Soul Urge, you feel fulfilled, authentic, and at peace. When it conflicts with your Soul Urge, you feel restless, unfulfilled, or like you're living someone else's life—even if that life looks successful from the outside.

## How to Calculate Your Soul Urge Number

Your Soul Urge Number is calculated using only the vowels in your full birth name. Vowels represent the inner sound, the breath, the soul—while consonants represent the outer structure and personality.

**The Vowels**: A, E, I, O, U (and sometimes Y)

**The Y Question**: Y is counted as a vowel when:
- It makes a vowel sound (like "ee" or "eye")
- It's the only vowel sound in a syllable
- Examples: Lynn (Y is vowel), Bryan (Y is vowel), Yolanda (Y is consonant), Amy (Y is vowel)

**Numerology Chart for Vowels**:
```
A = 1
E = 5
I = 9
O = 6
U = 3
Y = 7 (when used as vowel)
```

**Step-by-Step Calculation**:

1. Write out your full birth name (exactly as on birth certificate)
2. Identify and circle only the vowels
3. Assign each vowel its corresponding number
4. Add all vowel numbers together
5. Reduce to a single digit (1-9) or master number (11, 22, 33)

**Example 1: Simple Calculation**

Name: **SARAH JANE WILSON**

Vowels only:
```
S A R A H = A, A = 1+1 = 2
J A N E = A, E = 1+5 = 6
W I L S O N = I, O = 9+6 = 15 = 1+5 = 6

Total: 2 + 6 + 6 = 14 = 1+4 = 5
```

**Soul Urge Number: 5**

**Example 2: With Y as Vowel**

Name: **LYNN MARIE ANDERSON**

Vowels only:
```
L Y N N = Y = 7
M A R I E = A, I, E = 1+9+5 = 15 = 1+5 = 6
A N D E R S O N = A, E, O = 1+5+6 = 12 = 1+2 = 3

Total: 7 + 6 + 3 = 16 = 1+6 = 7
```

**Soul Urge Number: 7**

**Example 3: Master Number Result**

Name: **DANIEL ANTHONY WILLIAMS**

Vowels only:
```
D A N I E L = A, I, E = 1+9+5 = 15 = 1+5 = 6
A N T H O N Y = A, O, Y = 1+6+7 = 14 = 1+4 = 5
W I L L I A M S = I, I, A = 9+9+1 = 19 = 1+9 = 10 = 1+0 = 1

Total: 6 + 5 + 1 = 12 = 1+2 = 3
```

**Soul Urge Number: 3**

**Example 4: Getting Master 11**

Name: **OLIVIA GRACE THOMPSON**

Vowels only:
```
O L I V I A = O, I, I, A = 6+9+9+1 = 25 = 2+5 = 7
G R A C E = A, E = 1+5 = 6
T H O M P S O N = O, O = 6+6 = 12 = 1+2 = 3

Total: 7 + 6 + 3 = 16 = 1+6 = 7
```

**Soul Urge Number: 7**

**Pro Tip**: The Y as vowel can be tricky. When in doubt, try the calculation both ways and see which result resonates more with your inner truth.

## Soul Urge vs. Other Core Numbers

Understanding how your Soul Urge relates to your other numbers creates a complete picture:

**Soul Urge + Life Path**: Your Life Path is your journey; Soul Urge is what you desire on that journey. When aligned, life flows beautifully. When in conflict, you feel pulled in different directions.

**Soul Urge + Expression**: Expression is what you're talented at; Soul Urge is what you want to do. Best case: you're talented at what you desire. Challenge: desiring what you're not naturally gifted at.

**Soul Urge + Personality**: Personality is how others see you; Soul Urge is your private truth. Sometimes these align; sometimes your inner desires are completely different from your outer presentation.

Example: Life Path 4 (builder) with Soul Urge 5 (freedom) creates inner tension between the need for security and the desire for freedom. Growth involves finding ways to build stability while incorporating adventure and variety.

## Honoring Your Soul Urge

To live authentically and feel fulfilled:

**1. Acknowledge Your True Desires**: Stop pretending you want what you "should" want. Admit what you actually desire, even if it seems impractical or selfish.

**2. Create Space for Your Soul Urge**: Build regular practices, activities, or environments that satisfy your Soul Urge, even if they don't "produce" anything tangible.

**3. Make Career Choices That Honor It**: When possible, choose work that satisfies your Soul Urge, not just your skills or financial needs.

**4. Communicate Your Needs**: Let important people know what truly fulfills you so they can support rather than inadvertently block your Soul Urge.

**5. Balance with Other Numbers**: Honor your Soul Urge while also walking your Life Path and expressing your talents. Integration, not one-dimensional living.
