- Skilled trades (mastering craft through practice)

The Bull has extraordinary patience for mastery. While others are jumping from opportunity to opportunity, Taurus is quietly becoming the best at what they do. They understand that expertise is built through repetition, that quality takes time, that there are no shortcuts to genuine skill.
